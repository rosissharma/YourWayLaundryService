This is how Your Way Laundry Service works

The process is completed with 4 easy steps:

1. Set up your service

  • Select a 12-week or “as needed” contract
  • Give us your laundry special requests
  • Select a pick-up and drop-off location

2. We pick up your laundry

  • Use our custom laundry bag
  • Make sure your name is on the included tag
  • Leave your laundry at your desired location on your scheduled pick-up day
  • We will call or text you to let you know your laundry was picked up

3. Laundry done your way

  • We use high quality detergent & dryer sheets or products you provide. Our standard options:
    • o Our regular products
    o Hypoallergenic products
    o Eco-friendly products
    o Customer-provided products
  • We follow your special requests
  • We dry your clothes on medium heat

4. 48-hour delivery

  • Your cleaned, neatly folded and packaged clothes are back to you within 48 hours
  • Same day, ASAP service is available for an additional charge
  • Ironing available for an additional per-item charge
  • We will call or text you to let you know your laundry was dropped off
